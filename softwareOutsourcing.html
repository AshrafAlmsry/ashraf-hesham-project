The best engineers working fully integrated in your team, according to your processes and managed by your leaders.

Managed Team

A team working autonomously but communicating and reporting status as often as requested by the client. We provides all the roles needed to complete the team required by the client. This team is typically composed by PM, SW engineers, QA engineers and other roles that are defined for each specific project. The whole team interacts as often as needed in order to ensure the best results at delivery time.
